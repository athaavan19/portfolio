About Me

I’m Athaavan Ganisen, also known as Benny, a Creative Multimedia student at the University of Cyberjaya with a passion for visual storytelling and creative design. I specialize in video editing, cinematography, videography, and photography, creating visuals that are both impactful and meaningful. Skilled in Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, Photoshop, Illustrator, Lightroom, Figma, Canva, and Microsoft tools, I combine creativity with technical skills to bring ideas to life. I’m a detail-oriented and creative thinker who believes great design is not just about visuals, but also emotion, communication, and purpose.
